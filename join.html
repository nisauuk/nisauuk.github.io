Volunteer for Us

Interested in joining this unique movement? If you have what it takes to work for the only organisation of its kind in the world, then write to us! We recruit all year round, and tailor our posts with the individual volunteer in mind, so that we can help you reach your goals quicker. NISAU provides you with the opportunity to contribute in a unique way to our communities and to our two great nations. Whether it is India's socioeconomic development or the celebration of India's culture in the United Kingdom or the exchange and knowledge transfer of best practise in your field that floats your boat, NISAU provides you with opportunities galore. From events management to policymaking, from graphic design to international delegation management, we have roles for everyone. At NISAU, your role is what you make of it, and the sky is truly the limit.

Write to us at team@nisu.org.uk with your CV, stating the area of work you are interested in. If you are interested in doing a bit of everything, then please state you are applying for the post of National Officer.

Please note that all roles at NISAU are voluntary. Please be aware that you will be asked to declare any potential conflicts of interest e.g. business interests, political affiliations etc. as part of the joining process. In return for your time and efforts, volunteers are provided with lots of gratitude, creative satisfaction, networking opportunities, references and the experience of a lifetime!
