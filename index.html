Home of all Indian students and youth in the UK

Championing India to the World



Who we are

We are the oldest and largest network of Indian students, alumni and young professionals in the UK. We are your home away from home.

What we do

We help Indian students, represent them worldwide, organise the finest India focused events, and put Indian students at the heart of India-UK relations.

Our Events

We have a long history of organising some of the most thought-provoking, interesting and enjoyable events ranging from critical conversation to a celebration of Indian Culture.

Celebrating 75 years of India’s independence and NISAU’s 10th anniversary

We'll be running a series of events and activities as part of our celebrations.
